Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Lorie ("we", "our", or "us") is a calorie and nutrition tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address and password used to create your account.
  • Health and nutrition data: meals, calorie counts, macro targets, and nutrition history you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: app activity such as features used and session information, used to improve the app.
  • Purchase information: subscription status managed through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your nutrition tracking data across sessions.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To improve app performance and features.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. Data is stored on servers in the European Union. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – database and authentication (privacy policy).
  • OpenAI – used to analyze meal photos and estimate nutrition (privacy policy).
  • RevenueCat – subscription management (privacy policy).
  • Apple App Store – payment processing for subscriptions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the services listed above or as required by law.

Children's Privacy

Lorie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. To delete your account and data, contact us at the email below.
