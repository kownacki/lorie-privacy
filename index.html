Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Lorie ("we", "our", or "us") is a calorie and nutrition tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address provided through Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple when you create or access your account.
  • Meal photos: photos you take or select from your photo library for AI-based nutrition analysis. Photos are uploaded to our servers and sent to OpenAI for analysis.
  • Health and nutrition data: meals, calorie counts, macro targets, and nutrition history you enter into the app.
  • Purchase and subscription information: subscription status, App Store transaction or receipt data, and related identifiers processed to verify premium access. We do not store full payment card details.

Device Permissions

  • Camera: used to take meal photos for AI nutrition analysis. Access is requested only when you initiate a photo scan.
  • Photo library: used to select existing photos for AI nutrition analysis. Access is requested only when you choose to upload a photo.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync your nutrition tracking data across sessions.
  • To analyse meal photos and estimate nutritional content using AI.
  • To manage your account and subscription.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase on servers in the European Union. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – database, authentication, and photo storage (privacy policy).
  • OpenAI – used to analyse meal photos and estimate nutrition (privacy policy).
  • RevenueCat – subscription management and entitlement verification. RevenueCat may process app user identifiers, device technical information, and App Store transaction or receipt data to determine subscription status (privacy policy).
  • Apple App Store – payment processing for subscriptions.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the services listed above or as required by law.

Children's Privacy

Lorie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, use the Delete Account option in the app's Settings screen. Deletion is permanent and removes all your data from our servers.
